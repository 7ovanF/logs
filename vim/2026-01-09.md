CHPT 2
-IMPORTANT:
 operator [number] motion
 operator: like d (delete)
 [number]: optional, how many repetition
 motion: what do. (ex w, e, $ (end of line))
 motion ends the macro.
 if no operator, just [number] motion: repeat motion [n] times

-d (delete:operator) actually cuts, not just simple delete
 dd is special to delete line
-0 (start of line:motion)
-$ (end of line:motion)
-u (undo)
-U (undo all changes on line)
-ctrl+r (redo)


CHPT 3
-p (put/paste) put yanked or prevsly deleted.
-r[x] (replace) replace selected with letter x
-c (change:operator)
 cc: change wholeline


CHPT 4: Advanced motions (and some commands)
-G (go:motion)
 just G will take to file end
 gg will take to file beginning
 [n]G will take to line n
++ ctrl+G will give line data (how many lines in file, and where u are rn (if ruler off))

-/[phrase] (search:command) (? will just go backwards)
 n to go to next
 N to go to previous

-ctrl+o (motion) to go back before big movements like G or search
-ctrl+i (motion) goes forward

-% to go to brackets, closing and opening (need more research)

-:s/old/new/g (substitute:command)
 replace old string with new
 g (global) to substitute all in line
 c (confirm) to ask confirmation before changing (y/n/smth smth)
 :#,#s/old/new/.. to replace in lines from # to # 
 :%s/old/new/.. to replace in whole file
