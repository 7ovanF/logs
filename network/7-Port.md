### Portscanning [(Wikipedia: Port scanner)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Port_scanner)

Portscan: process that sends client requests to a range of server port addresses on a host, with the goal of finding an active port. Usually not used to attack, but to determine services available on a remote machine.

Portsweep: act of scanning multiple hosts for a specific port (reverse?)
